Key Features

All-in-one wellness, on your finger

ECG + AI Heart Analysis

Goodix 3228T ECG sensor with AI-powered heart health analysis. Spot-check ECG on demand, view trends in the HealthWear app.

PPG: SpO2, BP, Heart Rate, Temp

Goodix 3228T PPG sensor with dual SFH2703 photodiodes. True blood oxygen, photoelectric blood pressure, continuous heart rate, and skin temperature — 24/7 monitoring plus on-demand spot checks.

Sleep & Activity Tracking

Sleep stage analysis (light, deep, REM) with overnight SpO2. Steps, distance, calories tracked via STK8321 accelerometer. 8 sport modes including running, cycling, yoga, and swimming.

Family Care & Remote Monitoring

Add family members in the HealthWear app. View their health data remotely — heart rate, sleep, activity — in real time. Useful for caregivers monitoring elderly relatives.

4–6 Day Battery, Magnetic Charging

18mAh lithium polymer battery. 4–6 days active use, 10–15 days standby. Full charge in 1–2 hours via magnetic charging cable. No pins to corrode, no case to lose.

IP68 Waterproof, Built for 24/7 Wear

Epoxy resin + 304 stainless steel with anti-scratch matte finish. IP68 rated — shower, swim, and sleep with it on. Weighs 3.6–4.2g; most users forget they're wearing it.
